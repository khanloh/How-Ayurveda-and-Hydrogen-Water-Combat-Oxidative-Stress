Deoxidation of the Body: How Ayurveda and Hydrogen Water Combat Oxidative Stress

Oxidative stress is a condition caused by an imbalance between free radicals (reactive oxygen species, ROS) and the body's ability to neutralize them. Excessive oxidative stress damages cells, accelerates aging, and contributes to chronic diseases like cancer, diabetes, heart disease, and neurodegenerative disorders.

Modern science and ancient Ayurveda emphasize the importance of reducing oxidative stress to maintain optimal health. One emerging solution is hydrogen-rich water, which acts as a powerful antioxidant, aligning with Ayurvedic principles of detoxification and rejuvenation.

Oxidative Stress in Ayurveda: The Battle Between Ojas and Ama

Ayurveda describes oxidative stress as an imbalance between Ojas (vital life energy) and Ama (toxins/metabolic waste).

- Ojas: The essence of nutrition and digestion that provides immunity, longevity, and strength. It is similar to the body's natural defense against oxidative damage.
- Ama: Undigested metabolic waste that accumulates in the body, leading to disease. This aligns with the modern concept of free radical damage and oxidative stress.

When Ama builds up, it disrupts cellular function, weakens immunity, and accelerates aging—effects identical to those caused by oxidative stress.

- Yogasanas & Meditation: Pranayama (breathing exercises) and meditation reduce oxidative stress at a cellular level, improving Ojas.
